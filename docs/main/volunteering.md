# Volunteer Hours

Dalton has volunteer hours at the [Starkville Public Library](https://www.starkville.lib.ms.us/)
on the 2nd and 4th Saturdays of each month from 11am to 1pm (Central Time).

Some days he may miss or shift the time a bit, but check the [library's Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/STKPUBLIB/)
to verify he'll be there.
You can also [contact us](main/contact.md) to ask directly!
